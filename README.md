# 🛑 SubStop: Subscription Optimizer

**Never get charged for a forgotten subscription again.**

SubStop is a modern Android utility designed to solve "Subscription Fatigue." It helps users track, manage, and most importantly, **stop** unwanted subscriptions before the trial ends or the next billing cycle hits.

---

## ⚡ The Problem
We’ve all been there: signing up for a "7-day free trial," forgetting to cancel, and waking up to a notification that money has been deducted. With dozens of streaming, work, and lifestyle services, it’s impossible to keep track of every billing date.

## 💡 The Solution
SubStop provides a **proactive** approach to money management:
* **Predictive Alerts:** Notifications sent 24-48 hours before the hammer drops.
* **The "Kill Switch":** Direct deep links to cancellation pages (Google Play or Web) to save you from digging through settings menus.
* **Visual Urgency:** A countdown widget for Free Trials so you know exactly how much "free time" is left.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📅 Subscription Management
* **Smart Tracking:** Support for Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Yearly cycles.
* **Trial Mode:** Dedicated tracking for Free Trials with custom duration.
* **Total Cost Projection:** See exactly how much you spend per month and per year across all services.

### 🔔 Intelligent Monitoring
* **Next Payment Calculation:** Automatic logic to determine your next billing date.
* **Exact Alarms:** High-precision notifications that trigger even when the app is in the background.

### 🚀 The "Optimizer" Difference
* **One-Tap Cancellation:** Direct links to the cancellation UI of popular platforms.
* **Trial Countdown Widget:** A glanceable home screen widget for active trials.
* **Offline-First & Private:** Your financial data stays on your device. No cloud syncing, no tracking.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack (Android Native)

* **Language:** Kotlin
* **UI Framework:** [Jetpack Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose) (Material 3)
* **Architecture:** MVVM (Model-View-ViewModel) + Clean Architecture
* **Database:** [Room Persistence Library](https://developer.android.com/training/data-storage/room)
* **Background Tasks:** [WorkManager](https://developer.android.com/topic/libraries/architecture/workmanager) & [AlarmManager](https://developer.android.com/training/scheduling/alarms)
* **Dependency Injection:** Hilt

## 🔒 Privacy & Security
SubStop is built with privacy in mind:
* **No Accounts Required:** Start tracking immediately without an email.
* **Local Storage:** All data is stored locally using an encrypted Room database.
* **Zero Data Collection:** We do not track what services you use or how much you spend.
